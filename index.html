How it works

The bar is a single line from full Evil at the far left (-1.0) to full Good at the far right (+1.0), with True Neutral at the center (0.0).

Every time you wild shape, you move one fifth of the remaining distance toward the relevant extreme, so you drift gradually rather than in hard jumps.
